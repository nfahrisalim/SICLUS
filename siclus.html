SICLUS (Student Innovation Competition in Climate, Energy and Sustainability) is an annual event organized by SRE Unhas since 2023. This year's SICLUS holds the theme "Visioning the Sustainable City of the Future" challenging students to innovate, ensuring that future urban environments provide a high quality of life for all parties while protecting the planet. This year, SICLUS has expanded to an international level, following the success of last year's event, which was limited to the national stage. Collaborating with global institutions, SICLUS now engages participants from around the world, marking a significant step forward in its reach and impact.

Student Bootcamp

The Student Bootcamp is an immersive, hands-on training program specifically designed for the younger generation to gain new skills, enhance their knowledge, and prepare for the job market. This year, the event carries the theme "Youth for the Planet: Empowering Future Leaders in Providing Solutions for Affordable Energy and Green Mobility to Promote Sustainable Development."

Regina Farah Nafilah, our invited speaker for this event, will discuss the concept of being an "agent of change." Youth have significant potential to drive positive societal change, making it important to foster their abilities and initiatives in areas such as technology, politics, and economics.

Xpertise Talks is the grand finale of the SICLUS competition, presenting a special event featuring prominent national and international figures. This gathering collaboratively explored and formulated a shared vision under the theme "Visioning a City of the Future: Pioneering Innovations to Create a Future-Ready Metropolis." The event was also enlivened by captivating cultural dance performances and a spectacular concert, designed to entertain and inspire the audience.
